# Online courses that I recommend for learning to program

In this post I'll list the online courses I recommend for learning to program. I'll also include Youtube content I suggest following. My criteria for recommending them are mainly three:

1. I've taken them personally and they've been useful for me to learn.
2. They're widely recommended by the community and taught by high-level technical referents.
3. They're from universities or institutions of prestige, from where high-level technical referents have graduated.

Before starting, I want to make a disclaimer that the sooner you understand this, the better for the future:

> Most online courses are *passive content*, meaning they don't require you to exercise what you've learned to move on to the next topic. I make this disclaimer because I consider that exercising is fundamental to learn. <br/> With this in mind, I consider that they do serve as 'motivation' to keep discovering and learning.
